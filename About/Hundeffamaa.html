About ATSTO

The Oromia Science and Technology Authority (OSTA) is a governmental organization established by proclamation No 213/2019 G.C by the council of Caffee Oromia to work on science and technology-related activities in the region.

The following are among the mandates given to OSTA:

  • Conducting research on science and technology development needs of the region and its implementation;
  • Linking the science, technology, and innovation activities in industries, universities, research institutes, and TVET found in the region;
  • Organizing, coordinating, and delicately promoting quality infrastructure development (standardization, accreditation, metrology, and conformity assessment) in the region;
  • Standardizing traditional measurements for legal acceptance;
  • Establishing and supporting regional calibration and conformity assessment laboratories;
  • Collaborating with stakeholders on human capacity building of quality infrastructure elements.

Based on the above mandates, our quality infrastructure directorate works in the region to enhance the quality of products and services to ensure human and environmental safety.
